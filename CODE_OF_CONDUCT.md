# Apollo Client Code of Conduct

Oso and the Apollo Team are commited to perserving and fostering a diverse, welcoming community. Below is our code of conduct, and community guidlines. All applies to our repositories, organization, and everything in between. Everyone is expected to follow the code of conduct, no matter the affiliation, or position. 

To put it simply, communication should be:
- respectful and kind;
- about Apollo Client;
- about features and code, not the individuals involved.

# Be Respectful
Treat everyone with respect, regardless of their history, gender, ethnicty, etc... Build on each others ideas. Everyone of us has the right to enjoy our stay, expierence, and commitment without having to worry about discrimination, harrasment, or condescension, no matter how big or how small it may be. Everyone that commits don't all live in the exact same location. Everyone is geographically distributed and may not all speak the same language. We have all gotten frustrated when solving problems, but don't let it turn into personal attacks.   

We all should respect each others time. 

In general, while it is allowed to use AI for the development of this client (code authoring, bug finding, etc), you should not neglect discussing something with another human to an AI. If you upload an AI file, AI-authored CL, and a human responds, you're human too, so respond. Respond Respectfully and without the use of AI. Please remember that you are responsibile for all actions under your account. Everyone should hold themselves accountable. If an AI agent is using your account disrespectfully or unkind, the consequence of that is outlined below.


# Speak up if you see, or hear anything

You have the power to say something if you hear something that you don't like. Do so respectfully, and when you or others feel disrespected. If you do not want to speak up, or your concerns are not being fixed ethically, please consider contacting adminstration. We demand proof if any, as we do not want false reports. 

# Well will not tolerate the below, includind but not limited to:
**Communication (1)** 

- Harassing
- Intimidation
- Encouraging a person to engage in self-harm.
- Sustained disruption or derailing of threads, channels, lists, etc.
- Offensive or violent comments, jokes or otherwise
- Inappropriate sexual content
- Unwelcome sexual or otherwise aggressive attention
- Continued one-on-one communication after requests to cease
- Distribution or threat of distribution of people's personally identifying information, AKA “doxing”

**Coding (2)**

- Uploading malicious code.
- Failing to test code before commiting.
- Uploading AI-generated files without any sign of effort to write code
- Uploading plagarized code.
- Uploading files with inappropriate comments

# Consequences
Consequences for failing to comply with this policy may include, but not limited to, by the sole owner of Apollo Client:

- request of an apology;
- private or publicly warned
- a temporary ban from the Apollo Client repository, including any versions. 
- a permanent ban from the above. 


Everyone warned to behave correctly are expected to comply; failure to do so will result in an building-upon punishment.
